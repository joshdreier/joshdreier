Bio

Josh Dreier is a web developer and problem solver living in San Francisco, California. He has been building websites and digital media products since 1995, when he studied at NYU's Interactive Telecommunications Program. From 1999 through 2008, Josh worked as a Project Engineer and Technology Manager at Ogilvy Interactive in New York, where he managed a team of up to fifteen developers while leading technology projects for IBM, Cisco and many Fortune 500 clients. After leaving Ogilvy, he joined BBDO as a Technical Director for clients such as AT&T, Frito-Lay and Monster Worldwide.

In 2009, Josh became an independent contractor. He maintains a focus on frontend web development, offering his clients a wide variety of services, including:

  • Web Development (HTML5, CSS3, JS, JQuery, Ajax, PHP)
  • Web/Interaction Design (UX/UI/IA)
  • WAI-ARIA AA Consulting & Standardization
  • Responsive Design Development Techniques
  • Frontend Build scripts (Gulp, Grunt)
  • Source Control (Git, SVN)
  • Static Site Generators (Middleman, Jekyll)
  • WordPress, Squarespace, Setup
  • Experience working in CMS's (Drupal, AEM)
  • Experience with VM's (VMWare, Virtual Box)
  • Experience with Node.js
  • Experience with Javascript frameworks (Angular.js)
  • PM Tools (Jira, Confluence, Slack, etc)
  • Social Media API Integration
  • Coding for tablets, mobile devices (Android, iOS)
  • Analytics & Metrics Integration
  • Email Marketing
  • Video Compression/Optimization
  • Image Compression/Optimization
  • Search Engine Optimization (SEO)
  • Information Architecture
